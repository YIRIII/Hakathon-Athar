### Cost Sanity Check

| Check | Value | Threshold | Status |
|-------|-------|-----------|--------|
| Scale Year 1 ($2,250) / Year 1 Revenue ($6,692) | 33.6% | <30% (bootstrapped) | **Flag** — marginally above threshold |
| Marketing envelope from BUDGET_CONTEXT.md | $0-$670/yr (Bootstrap) | — | **Exceeds** at Scale tier |
| Bootstrap Year 1 ($0) / Year 1 Revenue ($6,692) | 0% | <30% | **OK** |

**Resolution**: At Bootstrap tier ($0 cash), marketing is within budget. Scale tier ($2,250) exceeds the bootstrapped marketing envelope ($670/yr) but is only activated when MRR > $500 (Growth tier trigger from BUDGET_CONTEXT.md). By then, annual revenue run rate would be ~$6K+, making $2,250 (~37%) reasonable for a growth-phase investment. The flag is acknowledged but acceptable given the stage transition.
