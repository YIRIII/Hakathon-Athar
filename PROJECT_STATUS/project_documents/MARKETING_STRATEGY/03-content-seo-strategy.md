## SEO Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Domain Signals | Content Volume | Content Cadence | Key Strengths |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Visit Saudi** (visitsaudi.com) | Strong — government domain, high authority | ~500+ destination pages | Monthly updates | Official authority; broad coverage; multi-language |
| **Islamic Landmarks** (islamiclandmarks.com) | Moderate — niche authority | ~200+ site pages | Infrequent updates | Deep Islamic site content; good long-tail SEO |
| **TripAdvisor** (tripadvisor.com) | Very Strong — global authority | Massive UGC | Continuous | User reviews, tour listings; weak on heritage depth |
| **SirahMaps** (sirahmaps.com) | Weak — niche site, low authority | ~20-30 pages | Infrequent | Deep Makkah content; poor SEO structure |
| **Wikipedia** | Very Strong — universal authority | Comprehensive | Community-edited | Authoritative but not interactive or experiential |
| **Athar (potential)** | New domain — no authority | Starting from zero | Target: 2-4/week | Niche authority opportunity: "heritage discovery Makkah Madinah" |

**SEO Strategy**: Athar cannot compete with TripAdvisor or Wikipedia on domain authority. Instead, target **long-tail, heritage-specific keywords** where existing content is poor quality. Build topical authority by publishing comprehensive heritage content for every site in the 10-12 MVP set, then expand.

## Content Calendar Framework

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Tied to Feature | Funnel Stage | Content Types | Frequency |
|--------|----------------|-------------|---------------|-----------|
| **Heritage Site Stories** | BR-2 (Site Pages) — Hero content | Awareness | Blog posts, social media shorts, "Did You Know" series | 3/week |
| **Pilgrim Heritage Guide** | BR-1 (Smart Map) — Hero | Consideration | "Complete heritage guide to Madinah", "10 sites near Haram you didn't know" | 1/week |
| **Digital Heritage Passport** | BR-6 (Passport) — Depth | Decision | User certificate showcases, "Complete your collection" challenges | 2/week |
| **AI Heritage Q&A** | BR-4 (Chatbot) — Depth | Awareness | "Ask Athar" Q&A series, chatbot conversation highlights | 1/week |
| **Heritage Maps & Discovery** | BR-1, BR-8 (Map, Nearby) | Consideration | Interactive map previews, "heritage sites within walking distance of..." | 1/week |

### Distribution Strategy

| Content Type | Primary Distribution | Secondary Distribution | Expected Reach |
|-------------|---------------------|----------------------|---------------|
| Heritage site stories (Arabic) | Instagram Reels, TikTok | Snapchat, Twitter/X | 500-5K per post (organic) |
| Heritage site stories (English) | Instagram, Blog/SEO | Reddit r/hajj r/islam, pilgrim forums | 200-2K per post |
| Pilgrim guides (long-form) | Blog (SEO-optimized) | Pinterest, email to pilgrim groups | 100-1K/month (growing with SEO) |
| Certificate showcases | Instagram Stories, TikTok | User reshares (viral loop) | Variable — depends on user sharing |
| Map previews | Instagram carousel, Twitter | WhatsApp groups, Telegram channels | 300-3K per post |

### Recommended Publishing Cadence

**Bootstrap**: 5-7 pieces/week (mostly repurposed from heritage research already done)
- 3× heritage "Did You Know" social posts (Instagram/TikTok, 30-60s)
- 1× long-form heritage site guide (blog, SEO-targeted)
- 1-2× map/discovery teasers (Instagram carousel)
- 1× "Ask Athar" AI Q&A highlight

**Growth**: 8-12 pieces/week (add video, user-generated certificate content)

**Scale**: 15-20 pieces/week (add Arabic YouTube, podcast, influencer collaborations)
